How we work

Four phases. Each with its own deliverable.

Engagements can take any subset of the four phases. We avoid the consulting reflex of converting every question into a six-month study — most engagements run between three and twenty weeks of focused principal time, with sustaining work where the client wants us to stay.

01

Assess

Digital and operations maturity assessment, leadership alignment workshops, baseline KPIs and senior-sponsor commitment. Deliverable: company assessment report.

02

Plan

Case for change, work-package scoping, vendor shortlist and quotes, roadmap and KPI plan. Deliverable: board-ready or funder-ready plan pack.

03

Implement

Vendor orchestration, on-site execution and oversight, change-agent coaching, issue resolution at the working level. Deliverable: live, stabilised systems.

04

Sustain

Quarterly KPI verification, executive coaching, continuous improvement, readiness for the next funding tier. Deliverable: sustained-results report.

Engagement shapes: Full programme (all four phases, sixteen to twenty weeks plus sustaining) · Diagnostic & roadmap only (Phases 1–2, four to six weeks) · Implementation support (plug into a programme already in flight) · Sustaining retainer (post-go-live).

Snorble — GCC market-entry strategy

Snorble Inc., a US consumer-technology company building an AI-powered interactive companion for early-childhood development, engaged Kontractom to design and execute its market-entry strategy into the Gulf, with Qatar as the beach-head.

We worked across three phases. The first sized and prioritised target markets and mapped Snorble's proposition to Qatar National Vision 2030 and NDS3 priorities. The second sequenced a phased funding stack — combining a non-dilutive research-grant pathway with an anchor equity investment from the local development bank — together with a month-by-month landing roadmap covering entity setup, partner contracting and first commercial milestones. The third refined the GCC commercial proposition into a dual-access offer — equity participation alongside an exclusive distribution licence — and sharpened the ideal-customer-profile definitions guiding partner outreach.

  • Landing-in-Qatar strategy deck, phased services proposal and refined GCC proposition pack.
  • Identified funding envelope and partnership architecture across institutional and corporate channels.
  • Positioned Snorble for funded entry into Qatar with a defined commercial wedge into the wider GCC.
