01 · Diagnostic Engine

The six dimensions.
One structured diagnostic.

PoE's rubric, applied consistently from the first engagement. The engine ingests every available source first — board packs and minutes, the value creation plan, IC papers, management accounts, steering committee notes, shareholder reporting, operational data — building a structured picture of what is confirmed, what conflicts, and what is missing. Stakeholder interviews then operate in three modes: confirm what the documents already evidence, validate where sources conflict, and elicit only what the record alone cannot answer. Senior advisor time is spent on judgment, not on chasing.

02 · Maturity Engine

From engagement to exit,
the improvement strategy stays live.

The Maturity Engine is the leave-behind. The improvement strategy the diagnostic produced — priorities, action paths, target levels for each of the six dimensions — is encoded into the platform on day one. On a defined cadence it ingests new board packs, operating reviews, and management data, matching evidence against the markers. When reality drifts from the plan, it surfaces the gap and points to the course correction. When evidence accumulates, it tracks the progress and tunes the recommendations. The senior advisor returns to a current picture, not a stale brief.
