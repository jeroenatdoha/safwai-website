The complication · what breaks today

The PIM already says the product is ready. It is the wrong guarantee.

BuildMart's PIM completeness check only verifies that a field is filled, never that the value is valid. Because each attribute holds one value for every channel, that supplier value is pushed identically to all of them.
Step 1 · clears the gate
A field is filled
The supplier feed declares a fire-reaction Euroclass. The box is filled, so the PIM marks the SKU complete. No one checks the value against the DoP.
Step 2 · fans out
One value, five channels
The attribute holds one value for every channel, so the same value is pushed identically to the .com site, print, shelf labels, screens and marketplace.
Step 3 · goes live
A wrong CE claim, public
On a construction product that means deactivation, non-conformity exposure to the French authority, and at the limit a recall.
The PIM confirms the box is filled. It never confirms the value is true, and on a CE claim that gap is a recall.
This is the silent break. Screen 2 is where we close it.Framing 2 / 4

Why this matters now

The Digital Product Passport for construction is law. The dates are set.

Construction has its own regulation, the revised Construction Products Regulation (EU 2024/3110), already in force. It makes a Digital Product Passport mandatory, and the milestones are dated, not hypothetical.
What must be in place
A passport reachable from a QR data carrier, carrying the product identity, the performance and CE / DoPC data, the substances (REACH), and the certificate references, registered with tiered access. Exactly what one validated record and a signed audit trail produce.
The dated milestones
8 Jan 2026in force
CPR (EU 2024/3110) applies. The DoP becomes the DoPC, including environmental data and the passport basis.
19 Jul 2026infrastructure
The EU central DPP registry must be operational.
2027 to 2028delegated act
Construction DPP becomes mandatory, phased in roughly 18 months after the delegated act that sets the format.
2030 / 2032phase-in
Core, then full life-cycle environmental indicators become mandatory.
Source: Regulation (EU) 2024/3110 (revised CPR); ESPR (EU) 2024/1781. Exact construction DPP date follows the delegated act; confirm against the latest act before the PoC scope is fixed.
Ready early means the validated values and the audit trail are already in place when the date arrives.Framing 4 / 4

Glossary

The terms on the glass

Every regulated or technical term used in this walk-through, in plain language.
Silent break A non-compliant supplier value clears the PIM's "is the field filled?" check and reaches the live site before anyone catches it. The pain this engine removes.
PIM Product Information Management system (Akeneo-class here). Holds product data and checks completeness; it does not check whether a value is valid.
Per channel ready An attribute that can carry a different value per channel. The opposite is one value shared across every channel, which is what the PIM does today.
DoP / DoPC / CE Declaration of Performance (and, under the new CPR, Conformity) plus CE marking. The mandatory conformity basis; every performance claim must trace to it.
CPR and hEN Construction Products Regulation (EU 305/2011, revised by 2024/3110) and its harmonised standards. The core jurisdictional rule set.
Euroclass The EU fire-reaction classification (A1 to F). A regulated value that must match the DoP, never be claimed beyond it.
R-value and λ Thermal resistance and thermal conductivity. The headline performance numbers for insulation.
REACH and SVHC EU chemicals regime; Substances of Very High Concern statement. A required safety and passport data point.
CLP EU classification, labelling and packaging of chemicals. Glass wool is not classified, so no hazard pictogram applies.
UKCA The UK marking that diverges from CE post-Brexit. Modelled here as the export and marketplace edge case.
ACERMI French insulation certification with a public register and an expiry cycle. The textbook "declared is not valid, check the register" case.
Keymark / NF / FSC Voluntary certifications. Keymark and NF are EU and FR schemes; FSC applies if the panel is wood-fibre based.
